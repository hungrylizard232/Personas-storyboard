Personas & Storyboarding

Project type: Course Assignment

Duration: 1 week

Team: Individual

Building personas and storyboard from interviews of users of treadmills in the Nelson Fitness Center.

Interface Introduction

The matrix treadmill uses a combination of touchscreen and physical buttons to help the users personalize and monitor their workout. It consists of a main screen, a phone charging station, and large buttons for speed/incline adjustment and emergency stop. The main screen contains the standard run status bars, as well as various applications like weather and instagram.

Recording Observations

General Obervations

  • Users start and end their workout with the same button presses, and they all seem very familiar with the start and end interface. For instance, they gesture towards the bottom left corner for the green start button, even though they are not currently on the start screen.
  • Users prefer to have some form of entertainment while working out on the treadmill, whether it is only audio or audio and visual.
  • Few users connect their personal entertainment accounts (netflix, spotify) onto the treadmill, because it seems more straightforward to simply use their phones.

Specific Questions

Do you use any type of entertainment while using the treadmill? If so, how do you use it?

Can you show me how you would adjust speed or incline during your workout?
